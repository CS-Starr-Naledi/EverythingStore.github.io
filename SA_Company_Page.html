About the Company

The EVERYTHING Store (with Forklifts) is a company that wants the best online shopping experience for its customers! It has top notch customer service, 20k+ 5 star reviews, and even a Michelin star just for having the tastiest decor?! The company was recently founded at May 10, 2025, when three of our creators decided to make a shopping website in pursuit of their dreams! This shopping project is still ongoing with our three leaders taking the lead in creating this website! But who are they?

Starr Dane Moreno

Starr was responsible for making this page, and designed parts of the main website! He also made the two categories, albums and furniture, putting up items for sale like the To Pimp a Butterfly vinyl and the Foldable Bed! We thank him for his dedication and hardwork for the website!

Edyn Monica Robles

Edyn was responsible for designing most parts of the main website! She also made the two categories, forklifts and stuffed toys, putting up items for sale like the 4 Wheel All Terrain Forklift Truck and Bread Capybara! We thank her for her dedication and hardwork for the website!

Lucas Francis Manganaan

Lucas was reponsible for coding the more technical parts of the website, like the add to cart button! Without him, ordering items in the website would not work and we would not be an online shopping website anymore, but just a plain text website! We thank him for his dedication and hardwork for the website!
